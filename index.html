About Karachi Revolution

Karachi Revolution Roller Skating Club is dedicated to promoting the sport of roller skating in Karachi. We provide a fun, safe, and inclusive environment for skaters of all levels to improve their skills, meet new friends, and participate in exciting events. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned skater, our club has something to offer everyone. Join us and be part of the revolution!

Passionate Community

Our club is built on the passion and enthusiasm of our members. We foster a supportive and energetic community where everyone is welcome.

Skating Events

From casual meetups to competitive events, we organize a variety of skating activities that cater to all interests and skill levels.

Inclusive Environment

We believe in inclusivity and diversity. Our club welcomes skaters of all ages, backgrounds, and abilities.

Roller Skating Workshop

A workshop to help skaters of all levels improve their techniques and learn new skills.

Roll Ball Classes

Roll Ball is a game played between two teams and is a unique combination of roller skates, basketball, handball, and throwball. It is played on "roller shoes" with each team consisting of twelve players, six on the field, and six in reserve.

Advanced Tricks Workshop

Take your skating skills to the next level with this workshop focused on advanced tricks and techniques.

Beginner Skates

Inliners for beginners are always delivered with a brake and have smaller wheels for an easier start. This results in a flatter and safer center of gravity with which you have a more stable stand on the skates and maktes the first steps easier.

Intermediate Skates

Intermediate inline skates offer a balance of stability, speed, and agility for recreational to advanced skaters.

Safety Kits

Hand pads, knee pads, elbow pads

Helmets

Helmets are designed to protect the most important part of your body. That brain of yours that likes to skate is very important to us all so be sure to keep it safe.
